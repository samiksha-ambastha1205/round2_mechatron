Session Instructions

Please read carefully before starting.

  1. The session runs in fullscreen. Do not exit fullscreen.
  2. Do not switch tabs/apps. Doing so will trigger warnings and can end the session.
  3. Do not resize or split the screen below the allowed threshold.
  4. Copy, paste, right-click, and developer tools are disabled.
  5. You have limited time. Keep an eye on the timer.

Clicking Start will enter fullscreen and begin the session.
